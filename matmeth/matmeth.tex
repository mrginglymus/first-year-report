\section{Cell Protocols}

\subsection{Overnight Cultures}

Prepare in a 14ml shaky top tube \SI{2}{\milli\litre} growth media with appropriate antibiotic.
Inoculate a single colony in the growth media and grow overnight at \SI{37}{\degree}.

\subsection{Inducing Cultures}

Prepare \SI{2}{\micro\litre} overnight culture. Prepare in a \SI{14}{\micro\litre} shaky top tube \SI{2}{\milli\litre} induction media with appropriate inducer. Inoculate induction media with \SI{20}{\micro\litre} overnight culture.

\subsection{Preparation of \ce{CaCl} Competent Cells}
Prepare \SI{2}{\milli\litre} overnight culture. 
Meanwhile, prewarm \SI{500}{\milli\litre} LB and two \SI{1}{\litre} or one \SI{2}{\litre} conical flasks and pre-cool a centrifuge to \SI{4}{\degree}.
Also cool 16 \SI{1.5}{\milli\litre} microcentrifuge tubes.
Add the overnight culture to the warmed \SI{500}{\milli\litre} in the conical flask(s) and incubate in a shaking incubator for \SIrange{2}{3}{\hour}.
Incubate cells on ice for \si{10}{\minute}.
Spin down cells for \SI{3}{\minute} at 6krpm. Resuspend cells in \SI{10}{\milli\litre} \SI{0.1}{\milli\molar} \ce{CaCl}.
Incubate cells on ice for \SI{10}{\minute}.
Spin down cells for \SI{3}{\minute} at 6krpm.
Resuspend cells in \SI{5}{\milli\litre} \SI{0.1}{\milli\molar} \ce{CaCl} in 15\% glycerol.
Alliquot \SI{300}{\micro\litre} into each of the cooled microcentrifuge tubes.
Freeze at \SI{-80}{\degree}.

\subsection{Transformation of \ce{CaCl} Competent Cells}
Thaw \SIrange{20}{200}{\micro\litre} competent cells on ice in \SI{1.5}{\milli\litre} microcentrifuge tubes.
Add \SI{1}{\micro\litre} plasmid DNA or \SI{20}{\micro\litre} ligation assembly DNA or complete Gibson Assembly DNA.
Incubate on ice for \SI{30}{\minute}.
Heat shock for \SIrange{45}{120}{\second} at \SI{42}{\degree}
Incubate on ice for at least \SI{2}{\minute}.
Add \SI{800}{\micro\litre} LB.
Incubate for \SI{1}{\hour} at \SI{37}{\degree} in a shaking incubator.
If required, spin down cells and remove excess LB to concentrate.
Plate out \SIrange{10}{100}{\micro\litre} on LB agar plates with appropriate antibiotic.
Incubate plates overnight at \SI{37}{\degree}.
